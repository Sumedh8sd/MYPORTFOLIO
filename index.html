Project 1

Description of Project 1.

Name : BANK CHURN MODELLING

Bank churn prediction model leverages machine learning algorithms to analyze customer data and predict the likelihood of a customer leaving the bank (churn).

By analyzing factors such as transaction history, account activity, customer demographics, and interaction patterns, the model identifies key indicators that contribute to churn. Using these insights, banks can proactively identify at-risk customers and take targeted retention strategies to prevent churn, thereby improving customer satisfaction and reducing revenue loss. This predictive model serves as a valuable tool for banks to optimize their customer retention efforts and enhance overall business performance.
